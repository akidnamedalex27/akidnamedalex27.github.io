THERE IS PROGRESS HERE

The Beginning

I have been drawing for at least 13 years now. At this moment I'm at the end of my last semester in College. YES I DID IT!! Now that I'm at the end, I can finally get back to work on some of my personal projects. This website is to show my progress in art and how much I've grown.

I worked on a comic called A-Lex Selvin: Neyon Warrior since I was ten. I made the series at first to be able to draw and come up with a story for a future video game project. I however, missed the part where you needed to be able to program games and ended up taking up comics since I started to like drawing them.

I spend a lot of time working on this one comic. I even had to rewrite the story 3 times. The first few issues was up to 3 comics, second time around was 12 issues of comics but had to be rewritten due to some issues with the plot, third time around barely lasted with only 2 chapters due to not having very good writting and not planning out the story first. Eventually, I got around to writing it for 4th time and by this time I had more experience in writing and art. I'm pretty happy at the level I'm at now and hope to keep growing as I keep drawing. I even have ideas for other comics I want to do in the future.
